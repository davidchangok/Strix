# Strix

**Version:** 1.1.0
**Author:** David W Zhang
**Repository:** [github.com/davidchangok/Strix](https://github.com/davidchangok/Strix)

## Overview

Strix is a lightweight, event-driven mailing assistant for World of Warcraft retail (Midnight 12.0+). It serves as your personal address book, streamlining the process of sending items to your alternate characters (alts) and frequent correspondents.

## Features

- **Quick-Select Menu:** Click the **▼** dropdown button on the recipient input field, or right-click the field, to open a context menu of your saved characters.
- **Automatic Alt Registration:** Simply log in to a character to register it in the "My Alts" list. Records Name, Realm, Faction, Class, Race, and Level.
- **Inbox Promotion:** Alts who send you mail are automatically promoted to the top of your alts list.
- **Recent Recipients:** Automatically saves non-alt players you send mail to.
- **List Management:**
    - **My Alts:** Drag-and-drop reordering. Delete entries with one click.
    - **Recent Recipients:** Promote a recent recipient to "My Alts" in one click.
    - **Display Limits:** Separate sliders control how many alts and recent recipients appear in the menu.
- **Event-Driven Architecture:** Minimal CPU and memory footprint.
- **Localization:** Full English and Simplified Chinese (zhCN) support.

## Usage

1. **Register Alts:** Log in to each of your characters once.
2. **Send Mail:** Open the Mailbox, go to the Send tab, and either click **▼** or **right-click** the recipient input field to select a character.
3. **Settings:** Type `/strix` or click "Manage List..." in the menu to open the configuration panel.

## Install

1. Download the latest release from [GitHub](https://github.com/davidchangok/Strix).
2. Extract into `World of Warcraft\_retail_\Interface\AddOns\Strix\`.
3. Restart WoW or `/reload`.

# Strix (中文)

**版本:** 1.1.0
**作者:** David W Zhang
**仓库:** [github.com/davidchangok/Strix](https://github.com/davidchangok/Strix)

## 简介

Strix 是一款轻量、事件驱动的魔兽世界正式服（Midnight 12.0+）邮件助手插件。它作为个人地址簿，极大简化了给小号或常用联系人寄送物品的流程。

## 功能特点

- **快捷菜单:** 点击收件人输入框右侧的 **▼** 下拉按钮，或右键点击输入框，即可呼出角色选择菜单。
- **自动记录小号:** 登录角色即自动注册到"我的小号"列表。记录姓名、服务器、阵营、职业、种族、等级。
- **收件箱置顶:** 小号发送的邮件会自动将该小号提升至列表首位。
- **最近收件人:** 自动保存非小号的邮件收件人。
- **列表管理:**
    - **我的小号:** 支持拖拽排序，一键删除。
    - **最近收件人:** 一键升级为"我的小号"。
    - **显示数量:** 可分别设置菜单中小号和最近收件人的显示上限。
- **事件驱动:** 极低的 CPU 与内存占用。
- **本地化:** 完整支持简体中文与英文。

## 使用方法

1. **注册小号:** 逐一登录需要记录的角色。
2. **发送邮件:** 打开邮箱，切换到发件标签页，点击收件人输入框右侧 **▼** 或右键点击输入框选择角色。
3. **设置:** 输入 `/strix` 或点击菜单中的"管理列表..."打开配置面板。

## 安装

1. 从 [GitHub](https://github.com/davidchangok/Strix) 下载最新版本。
2. 解压至 `World of Warcraft\_retail_\Interface\AddOns\Strix\`。
3. 重启游戏或 `/reload`。
